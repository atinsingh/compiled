Program Overview

Any emerging technology is high in demand, but data science is one of those fields that keeps evolving and generating a higher demand by the day. In fact, for the last 4 years, data science has been named the number one career field in North America. Few people are equipped to excel in data science, given that it requires a number of skills such as math, computers, and statistics. But Pragra’s data science program is designed to introduce even complete beginners to the field in a detail oriented fashion.
